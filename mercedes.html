About Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a German luxury automobile manufacturer known for its high-quality vehicles, innovative technology, and performance. Founded in 1926, the brand is a division of Daimler AG and is recognized worldwide for its luxury cars, buses, and trucks.
